SWIFT-Code: CITIGB2L\
IBAN Code: GB31CITI18500812207591\
Bankleitzahl: 185008\
Kontonummer: 12207591\
Kontoname: MICROSOFT

Bank: Citibank Europe plc\
SWIFT/Code: CITIIE2X\
IBAN-Code: IE11CITI99005170000123\
