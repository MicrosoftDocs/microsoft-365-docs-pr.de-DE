Voraussetzung für Minderjährige.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cd4d7-108">Businesses regulated by the CCPA will have a number of obligations to those consumers, including disclosures, General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)-like consumer data subject rights (DSRs), an “opt-out” for certain data transfers, and an “opt-in” requirement for minors.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="cd4d7-110">Das CCPA tritt am 1.
